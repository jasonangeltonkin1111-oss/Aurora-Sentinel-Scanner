# TREND PULLBACK / PULLBACK CONTINUATION

## PURPOSE

This file defines the core Aurora Trend Pullback / Pullback Continuation family.

It is written as a family-definition file, not as a final trading ruleset.

Its job is to make the family:
- structurally clear
- habitat-clear
- anti-confusion clear
- easier to translate later into wrapper logic, family cards, and EA logic

---

# 1. FAMILY NAME

## Canonical name
Trend Pullback / Pullback Continuation

## Closely related labels
- pullback continuation
- trend resumption
- retracement continuation
- MA-filtered trend family

---

# 2. CANONICAL IDEA

Participate when a directional market remains structurally intact, but the opportunity appears through temporary retracement rather than immediate expansion print.

This family is not about catching reversal.
It is about treating the retracement as temporary weakening inside an intact directional condition.

---

# 3. NATIVE HABITAT

Primary native habitats:
- Trend Pullback
- strong Directional Discovery that remains intact after temporary retracement

This family belongs where:
- prior directional discovery is already established
- retracement does not fully destroy the prior structural advantage
- continuation still dominates over balance re-formation or failed-state logic

---

# 4. ANTI-HABITAT

This family should not be treated as native in:
- full failed break / trap conditions
- reforming balance
- widening overlap / instability
- exhaustion or very late trend extension
- contexts where pullback is actually structural deterioration rather than orderly retracement

---

# 5. STRUCTURAL SIGNATURES

Typical structural signatures include:
- prior directional expansion already visible
- retracement that remains subordinate to the prior directional move
- no full reclaim of the earlier directional initiative by the opposing side
- signs that the market may resume continuation rather than rotate back into stable balance

---

# 6. MAIN COMPETING INTERPRETATIONS

Trend Pullback / Pullback Continuation must be distinguished from:
- Failed Break / Trap Reversal
- Balance Rotation / Range Mean-Reversion
- late exhausted continuation conditions
- raw Trend Continuation without meaningful retracement structure

The family is only valid when the retracement is still better interpreted as pullback than as failure.

---

# 7. MAIN ANTI-CONFUSIONS

Do not activate this family when:
- the pullback is actually a failed state
- balance is re-forming
- overlap / compression is growing instead of resolving
- opposing acceptance is becoming stronger than continuation trust

---

# 8. FUTURE PATTERN DEPENDENCIES

This family will later depend on pattern mappings such as:
- orderly pullback continuation structures
- post-break retracement hold patterns
- trend resumption structures after accepted retracement

These pattern children do not need to exist yet for the family to be valid as a family class.

- what Trend Pullback / Pullback Continuation is

---

# 10. CURRENT JUDGMENT

Trend Pullback / Pullback Continuation is one of the first core Aurora families because it is a natural extension of the current Wave 1 trend language and covers a major practical continuation habitat that is distinct from raw expansion chasing.
