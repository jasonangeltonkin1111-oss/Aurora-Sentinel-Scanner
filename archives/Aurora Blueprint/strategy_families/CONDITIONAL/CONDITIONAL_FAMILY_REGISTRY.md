# CONDITIONAL FAMILY REGISTRY

## PURPOSE

This file preserves strategy families that are real and useful, but should not yet be treated as clean first-wave core Aurora families.

These families are conditional because they need one or more of:
- stronger session doctrine
- stronger research/testing protection
- stronger cross-asset structure
- stronger macro/calendar handling
- stronger non-chart data support

They are preserved here so they are not lost while still being kept separate from the first core family pack.

---

# 1. CONDITIONAL FAMILY LIST

## CND-1. Intraday Momentum Family

### Canonical idea
Short-horizon momentum logic defined partly by session structure, intraday liquidity cycles, and time-of-day context.

### Why conditional
This family is real, but needs explicit session definitions and testing discipline beyond generic chart reading.

### Likely data surfaces
- S1 — Chart / Structure Surface
- S3 — Live Execution / Microstructure Surface
- S6 — Calendar / Session / Timebox Surface

### Future destination
- core candidate after session doctrine matures

---

## CND-2. Opening Range Breakout (ORB) Variants

### Canonical idea
Trade breakout or post-open directional resolution relative to a defined opening range.

### Why conditional
This is academically and practically real, but requires explicit timebox definition, session framing, and operational constraints.

### Likely data surfaces
- S1 — Chart / Structure Surface
- S3 — Live Execution / Microstructure Surface
- S6 — Calendar / Session / Timebox Surface

### Future destination
- core candidate after session/timebox doctrine matures

---

## CND-3. Cross-Sectional Momentum Family

### Canonical idea
Relative-strength / relative-weakness logic across instruments rather than isolated single-chart structure alone.

### Why conditional
This is valid, but Aurora needs stronger cross-asset grouping and ranking doctrine before treating it as active.

### Likely data surfaces
- S2 — ASC Scanner / Internal Feature Surface
- S5 — Cross-Asset / Relative Surface

### Future destination
- conditional family until cross-asset ranking and group logic are mature

---

## CND-4. Calendar / Seasonal Effects Family

### Canonical idea
Participation based partly on repeatable calendar or time-window effects.

### Why conditional
This is real but requires stronger research-method protection against overfitting and stronger timebox/event discipline.

### Likely data surfaces
- S4 — Macro / Regime Surface
- S6 — Calendar / Session / Timebox Surface

### Future destination
- later research-tested family layer
