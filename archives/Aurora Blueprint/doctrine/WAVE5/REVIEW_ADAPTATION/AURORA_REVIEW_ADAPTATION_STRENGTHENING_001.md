# 2. REVIEW / ADAPTATION STRENGTHENING ADDITIONS

## 2.1 Environment-fit rule

### Strengthening statement
Review should test whether a method fit the environment it faced, not only whether the method was generically “right” or “wrong.”

### Why this is justified
Adaptive Markets frames market behavior and strategy success as environment-dependent and evolutionary rather than timeless. fileciteturn101file1

### Canonical implication
Aurora review should explicitly ask:
- What environment was present?
- Was the method fit for that environment?
- Did failure reflect edge decay, environmental change, or execution error?

---

## 2.2 Adaptation-over-dogma rule

### Strengthening statement
Review should favor adaptive revision over dogmatic loyalty to previously successful methods.

### Why this is justified
Adaptive Markets emphasizes competition, adaptation, and natural selection as the drivers of market behavior. fileciteturn101file1

### Canonical implication
Aurora should preserve the principle that persistence of a method is earned by fit and evidence, not by tradition.

---

## 2.3 Operator-state review rule

### Strengthening statement
Review must consider the physiological and stress state of the operator as part of decision quality.

### Why this is justified
The Hour Between Dog and Wolf directly links testosterone, cortisol, stress, and overconfidence cycles to risk-taking behavior and judgment under pressure. fileciteturn101file0

### Canonical implication
Review should include prompts such as:
- Was stress elevated?
- Was confidence biochemically inflated?
- Was caution suppressed or exaggerated by body state?

---

## 2.4 Anti-hindsight rule

### Strengthening statement
Review must resist hindsight certainty and coherent-story reconstruction after the fact.

### Why this is justified
Thinking, Fast and Slow devotes major attention to illusion of understanding, illusion of validity, and bias in how we explain outcomes after they occur. fileciteturn101file2

### Canonical implication
Aurora review should preserve explicit checks against:
- hindsight contamination
- narrative rescue
- false certainty after noisy outcomes

---

## 2.5 Bias-pattern review rule

### Strengthening statement
Review should look for recurring patterns of bias rather than treating each mistake as isolated.

### Why this is justified
Thinking, Fast and Slow organizes judgment failures around recurring biases and heuristics; Misbehaving preserves recurring economic-choice distortions such as mental accounting and sunk-cost behavior. fileciteturn101file2turn101file3

### Canonical implication
Aurora review should ask:
- Which bias pattern recurred?
- What situation triggered it?
- What intervention could reduce repetition?

---

## 2.6 House-money / break-even review rule

### Strengthening statement
Review must explicitly test whether prior gains or losses distorted current risk appetite.

### Why this is justified
Misbehaving highlights the house-money effect and break-even effect as recurring distortions in human decision-making with money. fileciteturn101file3

### Canonical implication
Aurora review should check whether:
- gains made the operator looser than normal
- losses triggered risk escalation to recover prior pain
