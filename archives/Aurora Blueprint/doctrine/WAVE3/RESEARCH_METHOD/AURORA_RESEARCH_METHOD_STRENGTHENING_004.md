# 2. RESEARCH-METHOD STRENGTHENING ADDITIONS

## 2.1 Expected-return object rule

### Strengthening statement
Research should preserve expected return as an explicit object of estimation, comparison, and decomposition rather than leaving it implicit inside setup narratives.

### Why this is justified
Expected Returns on Major Asset Classes is directly organized around expected-return formation and the major premia across asset classes.

### Canonical implication
Aurora research should ask:
- what expected return is being claimed?
- what components drive it?
- how time-varying is the expectation?
- what background premia context supports or weakens it?

---

## 2.2 Cross-asset context rule

### Strengthening statement
Research should remain aware that setup quality does not exist in a vacuum; broader asset-class premia and cross-asset conditions can shape opportunity context.

### Why this is justified
Expected Returns on Major Asset Classes treats equity, bond, and credit premia as distinct but interacting return sources.

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve a cross-asset context layer in research notes and later ranking workflows.

---

## 2.3 ML-with-structure rule

### Strengthening statement
Machine learning should be used as a disciplined research extension for handling large information sets and ambiguous functional forms, not as a replacement for structural reasoning.

### Why this is justified
Financial Machine Learning explicitly frames ML in finance around information richness, functional ambiguity, return prediction, and the benefits of economic structure.

### Canonical implication
Aurora research should preserve the rule that ML models must remain tied to:
- clear experimental design
- structural interpretation when possible
- robust comparison against simpler baselines

---

## 2.4 Complexity-justification rule

### Strengthening statement
Higher complexity is acceptable only when it earns its place through robust out-of-sample leverage, wider useful information capture, or materially better decision support.

### Why this is justified
Financial Machine Learning gives major attention to model complexity, comparative analysis, and experimental design rather than treating complexity as automatically superior.

### Canonical implication
Aurora research should preserve explicit anti-complexity questions:
- what simple benchmark does this beat?
- what robustness survives?
- what interpretability is lost?
- what implementation friction is added?

---

## 2.5 Evaluation-near-implementation rule

### Strengthening statement
Research evaluation should remain connected to portfolio consequences, trading costs, and implementability.

### Why this is justified
Financial Machine Learning extends beyond prediction into portfolio choice, trading costs, and reinforcement-learning context.

### Canonical implication
Aurora research should preserve the rule that signal quality is not enough; implementation consequences matter during evaluation, not only after approval.

---

## 2.6 Scenario-pressure rule

### Strengthening statement
Research should remain aware that expected-return claims and model behavior can change under macro debt-cycle and order-shift pressure.

### Why this is justified
How Countries Go Broke frames larger regime pressure through debt, political, and geopolitical cycles.

### Canonical implication
Research/testing should preserve a context note for whether the environment sits inside broader fragility or order-transition pressure.
