# 2. RESEARCH-METHOD STRENGTHENING ADDITIONS

## 2.1 Benchmark-challenge rule

### Strengthening statement
Active-trading or active-selection claims should be stress-tested against passive or simpler baseline alternatives rather than judged only by internal coherence.

### Why this is justified
A Random Walk Down Wall Street preserves efficient-market and indexing logic as a persistent challenge baseline for active claims.

### Canonical implication
Aurora research should explicitly ask:
- what simple or passive benchmark is this claiming to beat?
- what evidence justifies the extra complexity or active management?

---

## 2.2 Simple-baseline respect rule

### Strengthening statement
A complicated framework should have to beat disciplined simple baselines rather than merely sounding more sophisticated.

### Why this is justified
The Little Book That Beats the Market preserves the pressure that simple ranking logic can outperform more ornate narratives.

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve simple baseline comparison as an anti-complexity discipline.

---

## 2.3 Source-of-edge realism rule

### Strengthening statement
Edge can come from source-of-observation advantages, institutional blind spots, and real-world noticing, not only from abstract model construction.

### Why this is justified
One Up On Wall Street strongly preserves observational edge and institutional-lag realism.

### Canonical implication
Aurora research should preserve a wider worldview about where edge ideas can originate, while still forcing them through evidence discipline later.

---

## 2.4 Bounded qualitative-insight rule

### Strengthening statement
Qualitative insight may matter, but it must remain bounded and explicit rather than turning into narrative rescue.

### Why this is justified
Common Stocks and Uncommon Profits is preserved here as a classic qualitative-edge source, even though deeper direct-text extraction from the uploaded PDF copy remains a later gap.

### Canonical implication
Aurora research should preserve room for qualitative edge, but only if it can be stated clearly enough to avoid story-driven drift.

---

## 2.5 Prudence-pressure rule

### Strengthening statement
Active claims should be pressure-tested against conservative discipline and downside awareness, not only against upside possibility.

### Why this is justified
The Intelligent Investor is preserved here as a prudence and discipline comparison source, even though deeper direct-text extraction from the uploaded MOBI copy remains a later gap.

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve a prudence-pressure check when evaluating active claims.
