# 2. RESEARCH-METHOD STRENGTHENING ADDITIONS

## 2.1 Finance-specific-ML rule

### Strengthening statement
ML in finance should be treated as a distinct subject with its own failure modes, not as a direct import of generic ML workflows.

### Why this is justified
Advances in Financial Machine Learning explicitly frames financial ML as separate from standard ML because of finance-specific overfitting, backtesting, and signal-quality problems.

### Canonical implication
Aurora research should preserve stricter finance-aware validation discipline whenever ML is involved.

---

## 2.2 Research-factory rule

### Strengthening statement
Research should be built as a structured production process rather than as isolated strategy hunting.

### Why this is justified
Advances in Financial Machine Learning contrasts the failed isolated-search paradigm with a meta-strategy / research-factory workflow.

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve process discipline over ad hoc brilliance narratives.

---

## 2.3 Anti-leakage / anti-overfitting rule

### Strengthening statement
Evaluation should explicitly defend against leakage, fragile backtests, and selection-driven false discovery.

### Why this is justified
The book’s chapter structure centers labeling, sample weights, purged cross-validation, dangers of backtesting, and strategy-failure analysis as load-bearing concerns.

### Canonical implication
Aurora research should preserve anti-leakage and anti-overfitting checks as non-optional.

---

## 2.4 State-aware-evaluation rule

### Strengthening statement
Some models and evaluations should remain state-aware rather than forcing all behavior through one stationary structure.
