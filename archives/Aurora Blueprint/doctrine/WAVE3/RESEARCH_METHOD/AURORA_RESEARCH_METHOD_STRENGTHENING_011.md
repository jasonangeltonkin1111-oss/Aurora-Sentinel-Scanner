# 2. RESEARCH-METHOD STRENGTHENING ADDITIONS

## 2.1 Adaptive-worldview rule

### Strengthening statement
Research should assume that market behavior adapts under competition and changing ecological conditions rather than remaining permanently stable.

### Why this is justified
Lo explicitly frames financial markets through evolution, adaptation, and natural selection. fileciteturn115file1

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve stronger adaptation-aware worldview when evaluating whether an observed edge is likely durable.

---

## 2.2 Judgment-error-vocabulary rule

### Strengthening statement
Research and review should use explicit labels for systematic judgment errors when those labels improve diagnosis and communication.

### Why this is justified
Kahneman explicitly argues for a richer vocabulary for discussing judgments and choices. fileciteturn115file3

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve better error-language for research discussion rather than vague “good/bad call” framing.

---

## 2.3 Intuition-validity rule

### Strengthening statement
Intuition should be trusted only where domain conditions and feedback make expert recognition possible.

### Why this is justified
Kahneman distinguishes valid expert intuition from heuristic substitution and highlights that not all intuition has the same status. fileciteturn115file3

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve stronger caution against romanticizing intuition when domain validity is weak.

---

## 2.4 Causal-story-versus-statistics rule

### Strengthening statement
Research should resist replacing weak statistical structure with strong causal stories just because those stories feel coherent.

### Why this is justified
Kahneman repeatedly highlights that System 1 thinks associatively and causally, while statistical thinking is harder and often neglected. fileciteturn115file3

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve stronger pressure against overconfident narrative explanation without statistical support.
