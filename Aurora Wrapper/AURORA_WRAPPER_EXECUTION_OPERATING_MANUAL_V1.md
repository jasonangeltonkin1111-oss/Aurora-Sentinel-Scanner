# AURORA WRAPPER EXECUTION OPERATING MANUAL V1

## PURPOSE

This file deepens the wrapper's execution-side knowledge so a GPT using only `Aurora Wrapper/` can reason more faithfully without relying on hidden Blueprint access.

It is not a replacement for:
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_EXECUTION_PACK.md`
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_CONTROL_PACK.md`
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_BRIDGE_PACK.md`
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_PACKET_EXAMPLE_VAULT.md`

It is a densification layer for wrapper-only use.

Its job is to preserve the execution-side intelligence that often gets over-compressed when source doctrine is compiled too aggressively:
- what the execution chain is really trying to protect
- how to reason through the chain without skipping layers
- what each layer is allowed to decide
- what each layer must refuse to decide
- how to preserve ambiguity, downgrade logic, and no-card-valid outcomes
- how to keep family/pattern/opportunity/deployability/geometry/card meaning separate

---

## ROOT LAW

Aurora execution-side reasoning is a staged truth-preservation system, not a trade-signal generator.

The purpose of the chain is not merely to arrive at a card.
The purpose is to make sure that:
- upstream truth is not fabricated
- local structure is not overclaimed
- execution burden is not hidden
- geometry is not invented early
- card emission is not treated as mandatory
- automation boundaries remain honest

A wrapper-only GPT should therefore treat the execution chain as:
- gated
- ownership-aware
- ambiguity-preserving
- downgrade-capable
- refusal-capable

not as a straight path from context to setup.

## THE CHAIN AS A PROTECTION SYSTEM

## Layer 1 — ASC context intake and normalization

### What this layer protects against
- fabricated upstream truth
- pretending absent surfaces are present
- treating degraded/stale surfaces as clean truth

### What this layer may decide
- what surfaces exist
- how trustworthy they are
- what remains missing, degraded, stale, invalid, unavailable, or unsupported

### What this layer must not decide
- family winner
- pattern winner
- deployability
- geometry
- tradeability

### Wrapper-only reminder
If surface truth is broken here, downstream sophistication does not rescue the case.
It only hides the problem.

---

## Layer 2 — Group-context construction

### What this layer protects against
- symbol-shell thinking
- one-chart myopia
- family routing without broader context posture

### What this layer may decide
- group posture
- bucket posture
- regime posture
- routing and boundary posture

### What this layer must not decide
- local pattern identity by itself
- card issuance
- geometry specifics

### Wrapper-only reminder
This is where Aurora stops being a single-ticker chart reader and starts becoming an opportunity system.

---

## Layer 3 — Context interpretation

### What this layer protects against
- raw field reading masquerading as interpretation
- forced certainty before structural synthesis exists

### What this layer may decide
- market-state interpretation
- execution-surface interpretation
- rough structural posture strong enough to enter family competition

### What this layer must not decide
- final family winner by narrative shortcut
- local geometry
- card timing assumptions

### Wrapper-only reminder
Interpretation is broader than local pattern shape.
It is the context in which family competition becomes meaningful.

---

## Layer 4 — Family competition

### What this layer protects against
- pattern-first shortcuts
- trend/range generic collapse
- premature winner forcing

### What this layer may decide
- best current family posture
- contested family posture
- deferred family posture
- no-valid-family or invalid-input stops

### What this layer must not decide
- local pattern by itself
- deployability by itself
- geometry by itself

### Wrapper-only reminder
If family truth is weak, downstream pattern detail becomes fake precision.

---

## Layer 5 — Pattern competition

### What this layer protects against
- detached local-pattern labeling
- reopening family routing by stealth
- geometry generation from unresolved local structure

### What this layer may decide
- strongest current local expression inside a surviving family posture
- contested local expression
- deferred pattern posture
- no-valid-pattern or invalid-input stops

### What this layer must not decide
- that family doctrine has changed
- that geometry already exists
- that a card now must be emitted

### Wrapper-only reminder
Pattern truth refines local structure.
It does not replace family truth.

---

## Layer 6 — Opportunity inventory and ranking

### What this layer protects against
- collapsing everything to trade/no-trade
- throwing away structurally interesting cases too early
- false neatness through forced reduction

### What this layer may decide
- whether the case remains worth preserving downstream
- whether it is eligible, degraded, observe-only, execution-invalid, or structure-invalid
- whether multiple live opportunities should coexist

### What this layer must not decide
- that deployability is already known just because opportunity remains alive
- that geometry must now exist
- that card emission is mandatory

### Wrapper-only reminder
Opportunity preservation is where Aurora resists starvation without loosening standards blindly.

---

## Layer 7 — Deployability judgment

### What this layer protects against
- confusing structural interest with execution practicality
- treating one friction input as a full deployability answer
- pretending bounded intraday use is already justified

### What this layer may decide
- whether the case is practically usable now
- whether it is watch-only, degraded, unknown, blocked, or usable
- whether execution burden, hostility, horizon, or continuity block deployment

### What this layer must not decide
- that family or pattern truth was wrong merely because deployment is poor
- that geometry can be invented despite deployment weakness

### Wrapper-only reminder
Deployability is where many good ideas correctly fail to become usable opportunities right now.
That is not system failure.

---

## Layer 8 — Intraday geometry construction

### What this layer protects against

- fake precision
- pretending explicit execution fields exist when they do not

### What this layer may decide
- entry law
- invalidation law
- target law
- timebox law
- execution constraints

### What this layer must not decide

### Wrapper-only reminder
Geometry is earned.
It is not automatically available just because the idea sounds plausible.

---

## Layer 9 — Generated strategy card

### What this layer protects against
- treating every preserved opportunity as a card
- confusing doctrinal files with live output objects
- hiding missingness beneath a polished output shell

### What this layer may decide
- whether a bounded symbol/time/context-specific card may be emitted now
- which blocking class prevents emission when it cannot

### What this layer must not decide
- that the whole chain can be skipped because a familiar setup seems present
- that card existence means automation readiness

### Wrapper-only reminder
No-card-valid is often the honest output.
Aurora must preserve that.

---

## Layer 10 — EA-safe boundary

### What this layer protects against
- fake automation certainty
- premature machine action
- exposing narrative reasoning as deterministic execution logic

### What this layer may decide
- which fields are safe enough to expose to later bounded automation

### What this layer must not decide
- that narrative interpretation is machine-safe just because it is written clearly
- that a generated card means broker-facing automation is justified

### Wrapper-only reminder
The EA-safe layer is a boundary discipline, not a production-readiness claim.

## THE FOUR SEPARATIONS THE WRAPPER MUST NEVER LOSE

## 1. Family vs Pattern
- family = organizing engine
- pattern = local structural expression inside that engine

## 2. Opportunity vs Deployability
- opportunity = should the case remain alive downstream?
- deployability = is the case practically usable inside project limits now?

## 3. Deployability vs Geometry
- deployability = bounded practical usability
- geometry = explicit execution field set

## 4. Geometry vs Card
- geometry = executable structure may exist
- card = Aurora has decided to emit a bounded output object now

If these four separations blur, the wrapper starts generating fake certainty.

## CARD-BLOCKING INTELLIGENCE

A wrapper-only GPT must preserve that card blocking may happen because of:
- structure invalidity
- deployability weakness
- geometry weakness
- missing surfaces
- timebox dishonesty
- stage exhaustion
- stage lateness severe enough to destroy honest remaining path
- insufficient stage legitimacy for the relevant family/pattern posture

That means:
- correct family read does not guarantee a card
- correct pattern read does not guarantee a card
- interesting opportunity does not guarantee a card
- usable geometry in theory does not guarantee a card
