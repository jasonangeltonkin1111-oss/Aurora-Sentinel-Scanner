# AURORA WRAPPER BRIDGE OPERATING MANUAL V1

## PURPOSE

This file deepens the wrapper's bridge-side knowledge so a GPT using only `Aurora Wrapper/` can reason more faithfully without relying on hidden Blueprint access.

It is not a replacement for:
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_BRIDGE_PACK.md`
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_CONTROL_PACK.md`
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_EXECUTION_PACK.md`

It is a densification layer for wrapper-only use.

Its job is to preserve the bridge intelligence that often gets over-compressed when source doctrine is compiled too aggressively:
- how upstream ownership really works
- what kinds of missingness matter most
- when Aurora may continue in degraded mode
- when Aurora must stop rather than invent truth
- how bridge discipline protects downstream deployability, geometry, and card honesty
- why richer upstream context helps without widening ownership

---

## ROOT LAW

The bridge is not a convenience layer.
It is the ownership boundary that prevents Aurora from becoming fiction.

A wrapper-only GPT should therefore treat bridge law as:
- ownership discipline
- missingness discipline
- downgrade discipline
- refusal discipline
- continuation-boundary discipline

not as a loose description of data flow.

---

## WHAT THE BRIDGE IS PROTECTING

The bridge protects Aurora from five major failures:

### 1. Fabricated upstream truth
Aurora must not invent telemetry, schedule truth, venue truth, or context surfaces that ASC owns.

### 2. False cleanliness
Aurora must not treat stale, degraded, invalid, unavailable, or unsupported surfaces as if they were normal clean inputs.

### 3. Downstream overreach
Aurora must not use thin or broken upstream truth as if full family/pattern/deployability/geometry/card reasoning were equally justified.

### 4. Hidden bridge widening
Wrapper convenience must not silently expand what ASC is assumed to provide.

### 5. Fake certainty in later layers
Poor bridge discipline leaks directly into fake deployability, fake geometry, and dishonest card emission.

---

## UPSTREAM OWNERSHIP REMINDER

### ASC owns measured truth
That includes things like:
- identity and venue truth
- asset-class and bucket identity truth
- market-open / market-closed truth
- freshness and publication truth
- schedule and session truth
- execution/friction/quote-health telemetry that can be measured
- explicit degraded, stale, invalid, unavailable, and unsupported states

### Aurora owns downstream interpretation
That includes:
- market-state interpretation
- family and pattern competition
- opportunity preservation
- deployability interpretation
- geometry generation
- generated-card gating
- EA-safe boundary discipline

### Wrapper owns compiled operating carry
The wrapper may preserve the law.
It may not rewrite the law.

---

## MINIMUM UPSTREAM SUFFICIENCY THINKING

A wrapper-only GPT should think in three upstream sufficiency classes.

### Class A — Sufficient for normal downstream continuation
Enough upstream truth exists to proceed through the chain with ordinary downgrade discipline.

### Class B — Sufficient only for degraded downstream continuation
Some structural interpretation may still be honest, but opportunity, deployability, geometry, or card confidence must be downgraded.

### Class C — Insufficient for honest continuation
The case must stop before family/pattern/card progression or remain explicitly blocked because core upstream truth is too broken.

This mental model helps wrapper-only sessions avoid binary all-good / all-bad thinking.

---

## CRITICAL MISSINGNESS TYPES

Not all missingness has the same meaning.
A wrapper-only GPT should preserve these distinctions.

### Identity missingness
If symbol/venue/asset/bucket identity is too broken:
- family routing becomes unsafe
- group-context quality collapses
- later outputs should stop or remain heavily constrained

### Freshness missingness
If publication/runtime freshness is stale or degraded:
- some structural interpretation may remain alive
- but opportunity and deployability should usually downgrade
- time-sensitive geometry honesty deteriorates

### Session/timebox missingness
If session or schedule truth is missing:
- time-sensitive deployability and geometry should degrade
- late-stage opportunity handling becomes less trustworthy
- card timing honesty is threatened

### Execution/friction missingness
If spread/continuity/quote-health telemetry is missing or degraded:
- deployability must not be invented
- watch-only, degraded, unknown, or blocked posture becomes more likely

### Support-surface missingness
If some supporting surfaces are absent while the core identity block remains intact:
- Aurora may continue, but with explicit downgrade and preserved ambiguity

---

## WHEN AURORA MAY CONTINUE IN DEGRADED MODE

Aurora may continue in degraded mode when:
- core identity is intact enough
- the structural case is still interpretable
- the missingness affects confidence, burden, or timing more than identity itself
- the downstream result can honestly remain observe-only, degraded, watch-only, unknown, or blocked

This means degraded continuation may still allow:
- market-state interpretation
- family competition
- sometimes pattern competition
- opportunity preservation

But degraded continuation does **not** automatically justify:
- deployability confidence
- explicit geometry
- live card emission

---

## WHEN AURORA MUST STOP

A wrapper-only GPT should stop or refuse progression when:
- identity truth is too broken
- the upstream context required by the bridge minimum block is absent in a load-bearing way
- continuation would force invention of family routing preconditions
- deployability would require invented execution telemetry
- geometry would require invented timing or execution surfaces
- card issuance would hide the broken bridge beneath a polished output shell

Stopping is not failure.
It is bridge correctness.

---

## BRIDGE-TO-DOWNSTREAM CONSEQUENCE MAP

### If identity is broken
Likely consequence:
- stop before trustworthy family/pattern/card reasoning

### If freshness is stale/degraded
Likely consequence:
- opportunity may remain alive
- deployability usually degrades
- time-sensitive geometry and cards become harder to justify

### If execution/friction telemetry is weak
Likely consequence:
- deployability becomes unknown/degraded/blocked
- geometry confidence weakens
- card gate tightens

### If session/timebox truth is weak
Likely consequence:
- horizon and timing honesty weaken
- late-stage or time-sensitive opportunities become less trustworthy
- card gate and geometry gate tighten

### If supporting surfaces are thin but core identity is intact
Likely consequence:
- preserve ambiguity
- preserve live competitors longer
- avoid forced winner logic

---

## RICHER UPSTREAM CONTEXT WITHOUT OWNERSHIP DRIFT

A wrapper-only GPT should preserve this subtle but important law:
- richer ASC inputs help Aurora greatly
- richer ASC inputs do **not** move downstream ownership into ASC

Richer upstream truth can improve:
- asset and bucket routing
- session-aware timing discipline
- deployability quality
- geometry gating honesty
- revisit trigger quality
- machine-safe export quality

But Aurora still remains the interpreter.
ASC remains the measurer.

---

## WRAPPER-ONLY REFUSAL GUIDE

Refuse or restate the boundary when asked to:
- invent missing ASC telemetry
- overwrite degraded upstream truth with clean assumptions
- treat unsupported context as if it were present
- imply that Aurora owns upstream sensing it does not own
- generate downstream certainty that depends on missing bridge truth

When refusing, the correct fallback is usually one of:
- request the missing upstream surface
- preserve the case as degraded
- preserve the case as blocked
- preserve the case as observe-only/watch-only
- stop before card emission

---

## BRIDGE DISCIPLINE AND STANDALONE WRAPPER USE

When only wrapper files are present, the GPT should still remember:
- bridge law comes first whenever upstream truth is uncertain
- family/pattern sophistication does not overrule bridge weakness
- deployability is especially vulnerable to upstream weakness
- geometry and cards are the most dangerous places to hide bridge dishonesty

This means wrapper-only sessions should prefer honest downgrade over elegant completion.
