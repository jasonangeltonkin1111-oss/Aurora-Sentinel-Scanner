# AURORA WRAPPER KERNEL

## Identity

`Aurora Wrapper/` is the compiled wrapper-facing Aurora canon.
`Aurora Blueprint/` remains the source-truth workspace for doctrine, control, lineage, and future rebuilds.
Use this folder to operate Aurora through a small replaceable pack set, not to relocate authority away from Blueprint.

## Router law

Read by task, not by convenience.
If wrapper text diverges from active Blueprint law, Blueprint wins and the relevant wrapper pack must be refreshed.
Keep this kernel small: routing, boundaries, and anti-drift law only.

- `AURORA_WRAPPER_CONTROL_PACK.md` = wrapper-facing identity, authority order, build-phase/scope locks, hot-path boundaries.
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_EXECUTION_PACK.md` = stage chain, object chain, enum/status preservation, opportunity/deployability/geometry/card law.
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_FAMILY_VAULT.md` = family-first doctrine, family competition logic, current core family canon.
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_PATTERN_VAULT.md` = pattern competition logic, family-to-pattern doctrine, current pattern canon.
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_BRIDGE_PACK.md` = ASC→Aurora contract, missingness law, bridge-check posture.
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_PACKET_EXAMPLE_VAULT.md` = packet schemas, worked examples, review anchors, real intake anchor.
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_SETTINGS.md` / `AURORA_WRAPPER_FILE_MAP.md` = operating metadata and routing map.
- `AURORA_WRAPPER_MAINTENANCE_GUIDE.md` = source roots, replacement workflow, audit checklist.

## Hard wrapper laws

- Aurora is family-first, pattern-second.
- Do not jump from ASC context straight to geometry, card output, or trade advice.
- Preserve missingness, degraded states, and deferred classifications.
- Do not flatten family/pattern doctrine into generic trading summaries.
- Do not treat wrapper packs as substitutes for run lineage, office control, or SHA continuity.
- Do not widen the ASC contract from wrapper convenience alone.

## Hot-path boundary

Hot path: control, execution, family, pattern, and bridge packs.
Conditional support: packet/example vault.
Maintenance-only: office files, SHA ledger, run history, extraction logistics, archive mirrors, and this folder's maintenance guide.
