# 2. RESEARCH-METHOD STRENGTHENING ADDITIONS

## 2.1 Objective-rule requirement

### Strengthening statement
A candidate trading idea must be expressed as an explicit rule or rule-set before Aurora treats it as researchable knowledge.

### Why this is justified
Evidence-Based Technical Analysis begins with objective rules and their evaluation rather than vague interpretation.

### Canonical implication
Research method should reject purely impressionistic strategy claims.

---

## 2.2 Scientific-method-first workflow

### Strengthening statement
Aurora research should proceed in a clear sequence:
1. define the claim
2. define the operational rule
3. choose the evaluation method
4. test for error sources and bias
5. review results without narrative rescue

### Why this is justified
EBTA structures its material around methodological and statistical foundations before case-study signal rules.

---

## 2.3 Anti-overfitting rule

### Strengthening statement
Data-mining bias, data-snooping bias, and look-ahead contamination should be treated as first-class failure modes in Aurora research.

### Why this is justified
EBTA explicitly foregrounds data-mining bias; Chan highlights look-ahead, survivorship, and data-snooping bias as major backtesting dangers.

### Canonical implication
Every research workflow should include explicit bias checks before accepting apparent edge.

---

## 2.4 Framework-first design rule

### Strengthening statement
Aurora should prefer framework-first system design over isolated tactic accumulation.

### Why this is justified
Systematic Trading is organized around forecasts, combined forecasts, volatility targeting, position sizing, portfolios, speed, and size.

### Canonical implication
Research should build systems from coherent components, not from disconnected tricks.

---

## 2.5 Benchmark / drawdown / cost checks

### Strengthening statement
Every candidate strategy or rule should be stress-checked against:
- benchmark comparison
- drawdown depth and duration
- transaction costs
- survivorship bias
- decay over time

### Why this is justified
Chan explicitly emphasizes these checks in idea generation and backtesting.

---

## 2.6 Backtesting is diagnostic, not proof

### Strengthening statement
Backtests should be treated as diagnostic tools that can reveal fragility, not as proof of durable edge.

### Why this is justified
Chan’s backtesting chapters emphasize pitfalls, measurement limits, and divergence between expected and actual performance.

---

## 2.7 Calibration and scorekeeping support research quality

### Strengthening statement
Research quality improves when forecasts and expectations are explicitly scored and calibrated rather than left impressionistic.

### Why this is justified
Superforecasting emphasizes keeping score, updating, and learning calibration habits.

### Canonical implication
Research logs should preserve expected outcomes and confidence assumptions, not only final performance metrics.

---

## 2.8 Workflow support is subordinate to method discipline

### Strengthening statement
Optimization, machine learning, and implementation workflow are useful, but they should remain subordinate to method discipline rather than replacing it.

### Why this is justified
Quantitative Trading Strategies Using Python provides workflow support, but the higher-order research rules come from EBTA, Systematic Trading, Chan, and Superforecasting.
