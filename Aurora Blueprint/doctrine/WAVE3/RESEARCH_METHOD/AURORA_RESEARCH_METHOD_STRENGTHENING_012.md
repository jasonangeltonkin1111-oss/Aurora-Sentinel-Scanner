# 2. RESEARCH-METHOD STRENGTHENING ADDITIONS

## 2.1 Workflow-discipline rule

### Strengthening statement
Research should preserve a staged workflow from data selection and feature design through modeling, backtesting, and implementation.

### Why this is justified
Peng Liu explicitly lays out a model-development workflow and broad data categories feeding the final trading decision. fileciteturn117file0

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve stronger workflow visibility instead of treating strategies as isolated outcome artifacts.

---

## 2.2 Implementation-realism rule

### Strengthening statement
Research validity should include implementation and scaling realism, not only historical backtest quality.

### Why this is justified
Chan 2008 explicitly frames quantitative trading as a process of finding, backtesting, implementing, scaling, and risk-managing strategies as a business. fileciteturn117file1

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve stronger implementation realism whenever strategy viability is judged.

---

## 2.3 Simplicity-versus-overfit rule

### Strengthening statement
Complexity should be treated as guilty until justified when the simpler alternative is more robust and less vulnerable to data-snooping bias.

### Why this is justified
Chan 2013 explicitly favors simple and linear strategies as an antidote to overfitting and data-snooping biases. fileciteturn117file2

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve stronger pressure to justify complexity rather than admire it by default.

---

## 2.4 Finance-specific-ML rule

### Strengthening statement
Machine learning in finance should be framed as a response to large information sets and ambiguous functional forms while remaining constrained by economic structure.

### Why this is justified
Kelly and Xiu explicitly frame those as the two core conditions that make finance fertile for ML while also emphasizing economic structure and finance-specific challenges. fileciteturn117file3

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve bounded ML enthusiasm rather than generic ML importation.

---

## 2.5 Calibration-aware-stat-arb rule

### Strengthening statement
Stat-arb should be researched as a calibrated, exposure-aware process rather than a naive correlation or spread-screen heuristic.

### Why this is justified
Pole’s book explicitly includes pair selection, factor exposure, market impact, calibration, and reversion quantification as core stat-arb concerns. fileciteturn117file4

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve stronger calibration discipline when researching relative-value strategies.
