## 2.1 Context-before-signal rule

### Strengthening statement

## 2.2 Sampling-fit rule

### Strengthening statement
The usefulness of data depends on whether the sampling frame and data structure fit the research purpose.

### Why this is justified
Chapter 1 explicitly discusses how different data granularities reveal or hide different structural information and warns that improper sampling can distort understanding. fileciteturn97file0turn97file1

### Canonical implication
Wave 3 should preserve sampling-fit discipline as part of evidence design, not as an afterthought.

## 2.3 Longer-frame-structure rule

### Strengthening statement
Short-frame decisions should be interpreted inside a broader structural frame when the broader frame is load-bearing.

### Why this is justified

## 2.4 Value/condition-aware interpretation rule

### Strengthening statement
Interpretation should remain aware of value regions and market condition rather than treating price action as self-sufficient.

### Why this is justified
