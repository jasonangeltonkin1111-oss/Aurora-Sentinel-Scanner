### Strengthening statement
Aurora should continue treating chart reading and structural price behavior as a first-class source of family hypotheses.

### Why this is justified
The classical technical-analysis source is strongest when used as a disciplined market-reading language rather than as an indiscriminate pattern catalog.

### Canonical implication
Family generation should still begin from structural reading of:
- trend
- range
- support/resistance
- reversal vs continuation tension
- decision-zone behavior

### What this changes
This strengthens the legitimacy of the current chart-first family stack rather than weakening it in favor of prematurely mechanical strategy thinking.

## 2.2 Support / Resistance Should Be Treated as Family-Relevant Decision Zones

### Strengthening statement
Support and resistance should be treated as structural decision zones that materially influence which family is plausible.

### Why this is justified
The price-action source uses support/resistance as a core part of chart interpretation, not as decorative lines.

### Canonical implication
Family interpretation should explicitly ask:
- is price at a structurally important decision zone?
- does this zone support continuation, rejection, reclaim, or range logic?

### What this changes
This particularly strengthens:
- Breakout / Compression Release
- Failed Break / Trap Reversal
- Balance Rotation / Range Mean-Reversion
