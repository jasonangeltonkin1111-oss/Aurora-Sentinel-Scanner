Trend Continuation

BUILD_READY

# CANONICAL THESIS
Participate when directional discovery is already structurally established and continuation remains stronger than balance, trap, or reversal interpretations.

# PRIMARY MARKET HABITAT
- Trend Expansion
- strong Directional Discovery
- post-acceptance continuation

# PRIMARY STATE / SURFACE DEPENDENCIES
## State dependencies
- Directional Discovery State

## Surface dependencies
- Discovery-with-Trust Surface preferred
- Structurally Valid but Low-Trust Surface may suppress or downgrade the family

# REQUIRED DATA SURFACES

# HIGH-VALUE SUPPORTING DATA SURFACES
- S2_ASC_INTERNAL_FEATURES
- S3_LIVE_EXECUTION_MICROSTRUCTURE

# STRUCTURAL ENTRY LOGIC (HIGH LEVEL ONLY)
At a high level, the family becomes plausible when:
- directional state is already established
- continuation remains the dominant interpretation
- acceptance and directional performance remain stronger than reversal or balance re-formation logic

# STRUCTURAL INVALIDATION LOGIC (HIGH LEVEL ONLY)
At a high level, the family weakens or invalidates when:
- directional performance degrades materially
- responsive opposition begins dominating
- failed-break or return-to-balance logic becomes stronger than continuation logic
- extension becomes exhausted enough that continuation trust is no longer primary

# MAIN FAMILY COMPETITORS
- Breakout / Compression Release
- Trend Pullback / Pullback Continuation
- Failed Break / Trap Reversal
- Balance Rotation / Range Mean-Reversion

# FAMILY REJECTION CONDITIONS
Reject or suppress the family when:
- attempted direction is stronger than actual directional performance
- breakout is unresolved rather than established
- trap/failure logic is becoming dominant
- environment is too low-trust for continuation to remain the best read

# FUTURE PATTERN CHILDREN
- orderly continuation structures
- accepted break-and-hold structures
- shallow continuation pullback structures

# FUTURE TESTING / RESEARCH NEEDS
- continuation durability by environment
- distinction between mature continuation and late exhausted continuation
- interaction between ASC ranking filters and continuation quality
- role of live spread/liquidity conditions in continuation trust

# FUTURE ENRICHMENT PATH
Future books and extractions may add:
- better continuation-vs-exhaustion distinctions
- better environment filters
- stronger trend-quality diagnostics
- stronger execution-context conditions for trustworthy continuation
