# CANONICAL THESIS
This pattern group captures retracement structures that remain subordinate to an already-established directional move and therefore preserve the possibility of continuation rather than signaling failure or balance re-formation.

# PRIMARY MARKET HABITAT
- Trend Pullback
- retracement within intact Directional Discovery

# STRUCTURAL MEANING
The meaning of this pattern is that the market is temporarily retracing without fully surrendering the prior directional advantage.

# STRUCTURAL INVALIDATION / FAILURE LOGIC
At a high level, the pattern weakens or fails when:
- retracement becomes structural deterioration
- opposing acceptance grows stronger than continuation trust
- balance re-formation or failed-state logic becomes dominant

# MAIN MISREADS / COMPETING INTERPRETATIONS
- failed break / trap reversal
- full balance re-formation
- unstable overlap mistaken for orderly pullback
- late exhausted continuation mistaken for healthy retracement

# PATTERN REJECTION CONDITIONS
Reject or suppress the pattern when:
- the retracement is no longer subordinate to the prior directional move
- reversal logic is stronger than continuation logic
- environment becomes too unstable or low-trust for continuation to remain the best read

# FUTURE TESTING / RESEARCH NEEDS

- live execution friction during retracement phases
