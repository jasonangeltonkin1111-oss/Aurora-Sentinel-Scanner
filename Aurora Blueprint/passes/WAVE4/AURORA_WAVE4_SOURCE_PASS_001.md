# 2. CORE EXTRACTIONS FOR VOLATILITY / RISK MODEL

## 2.1 Volatility is structural, not decorative

### Extracted truth
Option volatility texts and volatility-trading texts treat volatility as a structural market condition with direct implications for price behavior, risk, and strategy viability.

### Aurora translation
Aurora should treat volatility as a state condition, not as a cosmetic statistic layered on top of structure.

### Why this matters
Volatility must affect:
- trust in signals
- quality of continuation
- fragility of patterns
- plausibility of mean reversion vs expansion

---

## 2.2 Nonlinearity matters

### Extracted truth
Taleb’s dynamic hedging framework emphasizes convexity, nonlinear exposure, path dependence, and the dangers of assuming smooth, continuous behavior.

### Aurora translation
Aurora risk doctrine should explicitly preserve:
- nonlinear payoff / exposure awareness
- the danger of assuming linear continuation of current conditions
- the possibility that small structural errors become large risk errors in hostile conditions

---

## 2.3 Implied vs realized volatility intuition matters

### Extracted truth
Option volatility literature strongly distinguishes implied and realized volatility as different but related perspectives on market uncertainty.

### Aurora translation
Aurora should preserve a broader distinction between:
- observed realized movement
- implied or expected risk condition

### Why this matters
This helps keep risk doctrine forward-aware rather than purely backward-looking.

---

## 2.4 Volatility regimes matter

### Extracted truth
Volatility Trading treats volatility as regime-like rather than constant.

### Aurora translation
Aurora should preserve volatility-regime logic such as:
- compressed / low-vol conditions
- expanding / unstable vol conditions
- stressed / hostile vol conditions

### Why this matters
This gives later doctrine a way to connect volatility state to structural signal quality.

---

## 2.5 Tail risk is real even when not visible

### Extracted truth
The Black Swan emphasizes that rare, extreme, and model-breaking events matter more than naïve statistical comfort suggests.

### Aurora translation
Aurora should explicitly preserve the rule that low visible instability does not prove low tail risk.

### Why this matters
This protects the system from false calm and fragile confidence.

---

## 2.6 Risk worldview matters

### Extracted truth
Against the Gods frames risk as a broader probabilistic worldview and a discipline of uncertainty rather than mere calculation.

### Aurora translation
Aurora risk doctrine should include cultural and conceptual rules:
- uncertainty humility
- anti-certainty posture
- respect for probabilistic framing without pretending to control all outcomes

---

# 3. CORE EXTRACTIONS FOR HOSTILE-ENVIRONMENT REALISM

## 3.1 Hostility is a real market condition

### Extracted truth
Taleb and Black Swan both strongly support the idea that some environments are hostile in ways that invalidate simple, smooth assumptions.

### Aurora translation
Aurora should preserve a hostile-environment category where:
- assumptions about continuity are weak
- exposures are nonlinear
- normal-state behavior is unreliable

---

## 3.2 Smooth models fail under discontinuity

### Extracted truth
Dynamic Hedging repeatedly emphasizes the practical breakdown of simplified assumptions under jumps, discontinuities, and real-world frictions.

### Aurora translation
Hostile-environment doctrine should preserve the rule that strategies and interpretations built for smooth conditions may fail disproportionately in discontinuous conditions.

---

## 3.3 Rare events are not negligible residue

### Extracted truth
The Black Swan argues that rare extreme events dominate outcomes more than standard narratives admit.

### Aurora translation
Aurora should preserve the rule that tail events are not an optional afterthought in risk interpretation.

### Why this matters
This keeps the project from optimizing only for normal-looking environments.

---

## 3.4 Fragility matters more than elegance

### Extracted truth
Black Swan and Taleb both imply that beautiful theoretical constructions can be dangerously fragile.

### Aurora translation
Aurora hostile-environment realism should prefer:
- robustness over elegance
- exposure awareness over comfortable simplification
- humility over overfit confidence

---

## 3.5 Volatility can degrade state interpretation

### Extracted truth
Volatility regime changes alter the reliability and meaning of structural patterns.

### Aurora translation
Hostile-environment doctrine should later cross-link to Wave 1 and Wave 2 by asking:
- does current volatility regime degrade the trust of this state or family?
- is a normally valid structure now hostile because the environment changed?
