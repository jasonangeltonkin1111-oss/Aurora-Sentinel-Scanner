## 2.1 Finance-specific ML is its own subject

### Extracted truth
Advances in Financial Machine Learning explicitly argues that financial ML is a distinct subject because financial data creates special failure modes, especially around overfitting, backtesting, and misuse of standard ML workflows.

### Aurora translation
Wave 3 should preserve the rule that ML in finance cannot be treated as a direct import from generic ML practice.
It requires finance-specific research discipline.

### Why this matters
This strengthens Aurora’s anti-naive-ML posture and supports stricter validation workflow design.

---

## 2.2 Research should be built as a process, not a lucky strike

### Extracted truth
Advances in Financial Machine Learning contrasts the “Sisyphus paradigm” of isolated strategy hunters with the “meta-strategy paradigm” of a research factory that mass-produces discoveries through specialized roles and process discipline.

### Aurora translation
Wave 3 should preserve the rule that real discovery comes from a structured research process, not only from individual brilliance or ad hoc idea chasing.

### Why this matters
This supports Aurora’s multi-layer research design and gives stronger backing for process discipline over isolated backtest heroics.
