## 2.1 Risk is not adequately captured by variance alone

### Extracted truth
In the preface, Vince explicitly argues that variance and semivariance in returns are rarely the real risk that traders and institutions care about. He states that risk is primarily the probability of drawdown/ruin and specifically the chance of touching a lower absorbing barrier on equity. fileciteturn98file0

### Aurora translation
Wave 3 should preserve the rule that variance-only or semivariance-only risk proxies are insufficient when drawdown and ruin risk are the actual decision constraints.

### Why this matters
This strengthens Aurora’s anti-naive-risk posture and supports drawdown-aware evaluation over elegant but incomplete risk proxies.

## 3.2 Portfolio construction should go beyond classical mean-variance alone

### Extracted truth
The contents explicitly move from classical portfolio construction and the Markowitz model into the geometry of mean-variance portfolios, then into the Leverage Space Model and why that new framework is “better,” implying a critique of flat reliance on classical variance-based construction. fileciteturn98file0

### Aurora translation
Wave 2 should preserve a bounded skepticism toward classical mean-variance-only portfolio logic and remain open to allocation frameworks that account more directly for leverage, path dependence, and drawdown.

## 3.3 Random-process awareness matters before optimization

### Extracted truth
The book begins with gambling theory, random processes, independent versus dependent trials, and mathematical expectation before later optimization chapters, implying that allocation logic must start from process understanding rather than from optimization alone. fileciteturn98file0

### Aurora translation

## 3.4 Drawdown-aware portfolio logic is more realistic than smooth-optimality fantasies

### Extracted truth
The preface and introduction repeatedly frame the real-world problem as maximizing return for a given level of drawdown rather than simply optimizing elegant theoretical objectives. fileciteturn98file0
