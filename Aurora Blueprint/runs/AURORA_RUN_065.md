## RESEARCH-GUIDED WRAPPER JUDGMENT

### What current best-practice research supported
- keep the system/kernel layer small and boundary-first
- keep doctrine in external replaceable knowledge packs rather than one giant prompt
- prefer stable medium-sized packs over a monolith or many tiny fragments
- use explicit section boundaries and pack roles so retrieval/loading can stay targeted
- keep hot-path doctrine separate from maintenance/run/SHA continuity surfaces

### What Aurora-specific truth overrode generic advice
- family-first then pattern-second law remains non-negotiable
- Blueprint remains source truth; Wrapper remains compiled canon only
- examples must remain anchors rather than doctrine substitutes
- the bridge contract cannot be widened for wrapper convenience alone
